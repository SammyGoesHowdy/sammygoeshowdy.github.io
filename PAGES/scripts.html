These are links to scripts that you can use on legacy ROBLOX clients. You can find instructions below.


Play Solo Script

Instructions (Online method)

Requirements: Novetus, or a trustcheck patched client.

1. Open up your client of choice (2007-2017)

2. Go to the "Command" bar (if you do not see it, then go to "View" > "Toolbars", then click "Command")

3. Go to the script of your choice above, then right click, then click "Copy Link Address"

4. Paste the link you just copied into the command bar, then make sure to replace "https://" at the beginning of the link with "http://" and press Enter

5. Enjoy!

If you're having issues, scroll down to the troubleshooting section.

Instructions (Local Method)

Requirements: None

1. Open up your client of choice (2007-2017)

2. Go to the "Command" bar (if you do not see it, then go to "View" > "Toolbars", then click "Command")

3. Go to the script of your choice above, then right click, then click "Save link as", then save it anywhere you like.

4. Go to "Tools" > "Execute Script", find where you put the downloaded script, and press "OK".

5. Enjoy!

Troubleshooting

1. "Error in script: '<name>' expected near '/'"

Solution: Do the local method.

2. "httpGet failed, trying again" when running the online method

Solution: You either forgot to replace "https://" with "http://" before executing the command, or your client isn't trustcheck patched. Try the local method
